Accomplishments

  • Earned Numerous Awards and Trophies
  • Holds or shares more records than anyone else

Achievements
Most points in single career (2857)
Most goals in single career(894)
Most assist goals in career(1963)
Made 50 goals in 39 games

But that's not all, for this is just a few out of a grand total of 61 NHL records, and holds many more unofficial ones!

However, those aren't the only things the Great Gretzky has earned, he also has 4 Stanley Cups, 10 Art Ross Trophies, 2 Conn Smythe Trophies, 9 Hart Memorial Trophies, 5 Lady Byng Memorial Trophies, 1 Lester Patrick Trophy, AND 5 Ted Linsey Awards.

Child Star

  • Has been dominating hockey since childhood
  • Everyone knew about the legendary young Wayne Gretzky

Childhood Feats
Started ice hockey when he was 2 years old
Joined minor leagues at 8 years old
Averaged 104 points per season, far more than his teenage teammates
Well known through many newspapers

He was also known for numerous other things, among them being that he joined his first major league at the young age of 16! He also single handedly boosted the morale and power of the not-very-good team that he joined: The Peterborough Petes.

But perhaps the greatest thing about Gretzky was, is that he never let the attention go to his head, which was incredible because it was so much attention. He never once slipped up and gloated, he never had that dark side of him where he gloated to his losers and then suddenly lost the game, he was just that in control of his emotions and was that good at hockey.

To learn more, go to NHL.com
