Averaged 104 points per season, far more than his teenage teammates

He was also known for numerous other things, among them being that he joined his first major league at the young age of 16! He also single handedly boosted the morale and power of the not-very-good team that he joined: The Peterborough Petes.

But perhaps the greatest thing about Gretzky was, is that he never let the attention go to his head, which was incredible because it was so much attention. He never once slipped up and gloated, he never had that dark side of him where he gloated to his losers and then suddenly lost the game, he was just that in control of his emotions and was that good at hockey.

  • Time: 30 min
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients
PERDUE FRESH CUTS diced chicken brests
Ball of fresh or frozen pizza dough
Green pepper, sliced
White button mushrooms, sliced
Olive oil
Steak seasoning
Shredded provolone or mozzarella cheese, divided
Onion, sliced

Step 1: Saute. Heat the olive oil in a medium skillet over medium high heat. Add the chicken and season with steak seasoning. Saute until chicken is almost cooked through, about 4 to 5 minutes. Remove from heat and set aside.

Step 2: Stretch. Preheat your oven to 425 degrees F. Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray. Stretch the pizza dough and press into the baking sheet. It will tend to spring back, but keep stretching it out and it will stay. Letting the dough rest for several minutes will also relax the dough and make it easier to press out.

Step 3: Top. Spread 1 cup shredded cheese over the dough. Top with the sliced peppers, onions, mushrooms and cooked chicken. Sprinkle with remaining 1 cup of shredded cheese.

Step 4: Serve. Place Cheese Steak Pizza in the oven on the lowest rack and bake for 20 minutes or until golden brown. Chicken is done when a meat thermometer inserted in the center reaches 165 degrees F Enjoy with a cold beverage!
